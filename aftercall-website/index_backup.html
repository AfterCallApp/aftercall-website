Our story

I built this because nobody else was going to.

EMS providers run into some of the worst moments of people's lives, every single shift. They do it with skill and they do it without flinching. And when the call is over, they document, clear the scene, and wait for the tones to drop again.

Nobody asks if they're okay. Nobody tracks what they're doing out there. Nobody built them a tool that actually understands this job.

That's what AfterCall is. I built it because I understood the culture, the burnout, the weight of a bad pediatric call at 3am, and the frustration of documentation software that treats you like a data entry clerk instead of a clinician.

This isn't a generic wellness app with an ambulance on the icon. Every single feature was designed around what EMS providers actually experience. The AI knows clinical language. The wellness tools understand compassion fatigue. The community is built around how crews actually talk to each other.

AfterCall LLC is an independent company. No corporate backing. Just a deep belief that the people who run toward emergencies deserve a tool that's genuinely on their side.
