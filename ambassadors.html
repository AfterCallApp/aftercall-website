AfterCall partners with EMS creators — students, working EMTs and medics, and established voices — to spread the word the way it should be spread: peer to peer. Your own code, real payouts, creative freedom.

What you get

A unique code tied to your name, milestone bonuses for free signups ($50 at 100, another $50 at 500), and a payout on every Pro conversion your code drives. Paid monthly via PayPal or Venmo once you pass $25.

What your audience gets

Anyone who signs up with your code gets a 30-day free trial of AfterCall Pro — AI call narratives, guided reflection, weekly insights — instead of the standard 7 days.

Before you apply, know what AfterCall is not

AfterCall is not a HIPAA tool and not a PCR/charting app. It's a personal wellness and reflection app for EMS providers. It never replaces your agency's ePCR and must never be presented that way.

No PHI, ever. Content you make can never show patient names, dates of birth, facility names, or any identifying details. Demos use mock data.

No medical claims. AfterCall supports wellness journaling and reflection. It does not treat, prevent, or manage PTSD, depression, or any medical condition.

FTC disclosure is mandatory. #ad or #partner on every sponsored post, in the caption or on-screen for video.
